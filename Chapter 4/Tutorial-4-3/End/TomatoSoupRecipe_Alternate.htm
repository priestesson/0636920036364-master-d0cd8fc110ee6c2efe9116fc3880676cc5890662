Simple Tomato Soup

A simple, nourishing soup that showcases fresh tomatoes.

  • 2 carrots
  • 2 sticks celery
  • 1-2 onions
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 lbs tomatoes, peeled
  • A few mint leaves

  1. Roughly chop the carrots, celery, and onions. The mixture doesn't need to be perfectly chopped, because you'll puree it later.
  2. Cook the vegetables in a slightly oiled stock pot for few minutes, until the onions are translucent.
  3. Add water until just covering the vegetables, and then add the salt.
  4. Heat the water to a boil. Then reduce the heat, and simmer until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Add tomatoes and mint. Using an immersion blender, blend the mixture until it has the consistency of a rough puree (not completely smooth).
  6. Bring soup back to simmer, then taste and correct seasonings if necessary.
